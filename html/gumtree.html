Below are images of the existing gumtree trading website. There are several issues in regards to usability so our group redesigned the site to address them.

Redesign Aims

The goal with my redesign was to address the usability issues wth gumtree. By constructing personas of potential users and then considering their needs several changes were made. The UI was simplified in many places to allow quicker navigation. New features were added to improve buying and selling experience.
